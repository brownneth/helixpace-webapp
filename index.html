Frequently Asked Questions

? What is a DNA?

Deoxyribonucleic acid (DNA) is the molecule that carries genetic information for the development and functioning of an organism. It consists of two strands that wind around each other to form a twisted ladder shape known as a double helix.

? What is Helixpace?

Helixpace is a specialized digital terminal designed for modern labs to input, validate, and analyze nucleotide sequences. It ensures data integrity by filtering raw inputs and providing instant metric analysis for batch processing.
